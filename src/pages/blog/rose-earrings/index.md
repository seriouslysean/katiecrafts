<p>Project: Rose Earrings</p><p>I love making jewelry, but there are times that stringing teeny beads using needle-nose jewelry pliers gets tedious. That’s why I like to switch it up sometimes with a very easy jewelry project. Resin cabochons, post earring backs and glue are all you need to complete this project- what can be easier?</p><p>Supplies:</p><ul><li><p>Flat pad lead free earring posts</p></li><li><p>Glue (Recommended: E-6000)</p></li><li><p>Resin Cabochons</p></li></ul><p>Instructions:</p><p>I’ve bought cabochons from several different vendors over the last couple years, but</p><a title="Snapcrafty" href="http://snapcrafty.blogspot.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Snapcrafty</p></strong></a><p>is by far my favorite. She has a ton of choices and you can’t beat the price! Just make sure whatever cabs you pick are</p><strong><p>larger</p></strong><p>than the flat part of the earring post.</p><p>As for the earring posts- I can’t remember where I bought them, but I’ve since found them on</p><a title="Lead Free Earring Posts on Amazon" href="http://amzn.to/1eGgW6u" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Amazon</p></strong></a><p>for approximately one bazillion times cheaper. They even come with plastic backs (my last posts didn’t, so I had to additionally buy earring backs)! I’ll be buying from there next time for certain!</p><p>The last supply you need is the glue! This is important. I’ve tried two other jewelry glues AND a hot glue gun, and everything always would pop off easily. I finally tried</p><a title="E-6000 Craft Glue" href="http://amzn.to/1ezXwSm" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>E-6000</p></strong></a><p>and I won’t use any other glue now. Plus it’s great on other craft projects, too- so yay! It does take FOREVER to dry, though. *Note: Forever = about 24 hours.</p><p>Just pick your favorite cabs, glue them to the posts (try not to get any glue on your skin!), and let sit cab-side-down in a safe place for 24 hours. By the next day, you’ll have an adorable pair of earrings ready to go!</p><p>Don’t have the supplies to make your own, or prefer a pair already made? You can buy navy blue ones like the pair above right from my</p><a title="Navy Blue Rose Earrings on Katie Crafts Etsy" href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/178668291/navy-blue-rose-earrings-resin-10mm" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Etsy shop!</p></strong></a><p>Neon green doodles not included. 😉</p><p>If you make cabochon jewelry too, what supplies have you found and loved?</p>
