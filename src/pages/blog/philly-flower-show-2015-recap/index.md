<p>As you know, last week the Husband and I went on our annual date to the</p><a title="The Flower Show" href="http://theflowershow.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Philadelphia Flower Show</p></strong></a><p>! It’s my favorite event of the year. It happened to be a crazy snowstorm that day, so it was basically empty, which meant we were able to take our time and examine pretty much every inch of it without crowds pushing us out of the way or a hundred people ruining each photo. The theme was “Celebrating The Movies” and it was wonderful.</p><p>We started off in the Make-and-Take room again, so that I could make a ridiculous gigantic flowery hair clip to wear around the whole day.
