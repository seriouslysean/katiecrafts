<p>What a lovely weekend! Friday was off to a rough start. We had so many issues with the car we rented for the day, we had to take it back and switch to a new car which put a huge dent in our day. We also spent a good hour at PennDot getting new licenses since Husband’s was about to expire. That gave me the chance to officially (finally) change my last name to his, so there’s that! We went on to spend the remainder of the evening wandering around New Hope (full post on that tomorrow!) and having an awesome sushi/hibachi dinner! Saturday was spent at Reading Terminal Market and reading books in Washington Square Park. Well, I read. Husband fell asleep! Today we’re going for pedicures (yippee) before what I hope is the continuation of a relaxing birthday weekend for the Husband. I hope your Sunday is wonderful, too! Here are my weekly picks- hope you like ’em!</p><h2>Makes Me Laugh:<span id="eow-title" class="watch-title yt-uix-expander-head" dir="ltr" title="Aug(De)Mented Reality"><p>Aug(De)Mented Reality</p></span></h2><p>What fun! Artist “Hombre McSteez” takes some transparent cels, a marker and white out to create the most wonderful animated cartoon creatures. Don’t take my word for it- go watch! Then follow him on</p><a title="Hombre McSteez" href="http://instagram.com/hombre_mcsteez#" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Instagram</a><p>for more fun!</p><br><h2>What I’m Reading: Reading Rainbow Kickstarter</h2><p>I really don’t back things on Kickstarter too often. Either I find the project to be totally ridiculous and absolutely not worth the money it’s asking for at ALL, or I simply can’t afford to. That being said, this week I backed a project by LeVar Burton and Reading Rainbow called</p><a title="Bring Reading Rainbow To Every Child Everywhere" href="http://kck.st/1kKwSrD" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>“Bring Reading Rainbow Back For Every Child, Everywhere.”</p></strong></a><p>I wasn’t the only one who felt it was worthy- it hit it’s goal of one million dollars just 11 hours in to posting! Just a couple of days later it has over 70,000 backers and has made over 3 million dollars. There are still 30 days left to go- let’s hope it breaks all Kickstarter records over the next month! Here’s why you should support this very important project, too!</p><h2><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/6b99fbfe3d0640614d23dc9b804642b9-large.jpg" alt="Sunday Funday: Issue 15 on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="1758">Place I Love: Tuscany</h2><p>How can anyone say anything bad about somewhere so damn gorgeous!? That’s why this week Tuscany is my place I love!</p><h2><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/vineyards.jpg" alt="Sunday Funday: Issue 15 on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="663"></h2><h2>Something Delicious: Reese’s Pieces!</h2><p>Sometimes I don’t want something complicated.
