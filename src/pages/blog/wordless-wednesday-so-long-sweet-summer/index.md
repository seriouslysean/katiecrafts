<p>Doesn’t this photo say it all? Lucky enjoys a last shred of Summer: corn on the cob, freshly buttered and hand-served via his Papa.</p><p>The end of Summer always makes me think of this Dashboard Confessional song:</p><p><em>So long sweet summer,</em></p><br><em><p>I stumbled upon you and gratefully basked in your rays.</p></em><br><em><p>So long sweet slumber.</p></em><br><em><p>I fell into you now you’re gracefully falling away.</p></em><p>Ahh, Dashboard. I think I’ll whip out my old albums and reminisce now.</p><p>What ways will you enjoy your last bit of Summer? Are you happy or sad to see the heat leave and the brisk air roll in?</p>
