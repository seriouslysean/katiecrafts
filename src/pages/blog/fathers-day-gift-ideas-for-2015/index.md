<p>My Dad is SO hard to buy for. His birthday is at the end of May, and then Father’s Day is just a month later, so I usually have a hard time coming up with presents for BOTH days. Luckily, Etsy has some really amazing choices!</p><p>My father doesn’t really like sports that much. He isn’t in to golf. He doesn’t have a fancy mustache that needs grooming or more ties to add to his collection (I think he currently has three total). He used to hunt and fish all the time but now he’s getting older and sitting in a tree stand for hours is a little hard on him. He still loves to go crabbing when he gets the chance, but that’s about it as far as activities go. Though he does love to cook and grill!</p><p>Most items marketed for Father’s Day aren’t relevant to my Dad’s likes, which always made the holiday hard. But like I said before, Etsy is a life saver. Still, I know if I bought that for him back when I was in high school, he would have worn it practically every day!</p><p>Father’s Day is Sunday, June 21st, so you totally have enough time to shop these wonderful stores!
