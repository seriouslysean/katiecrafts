<p>A couple days after our long, exhausting and amazing NYCC trip, Husband and I joined his family for a beautiful day of pumpkin picking, apple cider donut eating, hayride riding and more at Linvilla Orchards in Media, PA! We had such a lovely day and we couldn’t have asked for nicer weather, especially considering the day before was quite cold and dreary. We were very busy enjoying ourselves, but managed to take a few pics while there!</p><p>This time of year, Linvilla is packed to the brim with people and gourds for Pumpkinland! When we first got there, it was a bit overwhelming. It took a million years to find a parking spot and just as long to navigate the INSANE crowd of people swarming all over the place. It thankfully thinned out a bit (or maybe we just got used to the mob) and we had a wonderful day!</p><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">

        </div></div><p>The pumpkins ranged from very teeny to small and then from large to gigantic! It seemed all the medium, regular sized pumpkins were already gone. We will have to find one for carving up around the city, but we came home with a collection of 9 gourds and pumpkins of different shapes and colors, a little hay bale, an adorable metal crow decoration, and half a dozen apple cider donuts to accompany our gallon of apple cider. Don’t they all look cute with our scarecrow in front of our house?</p><p>I should have watered the plants AFTER taking pics, in hindsight.</p><p>Also, how creepy does our home look here? Perfect for Halloween!</p><p>The orchards had a lot more to offer than just pumpkins. Depending on the season you can pick peaches, pears, strawberries, pine trees, apples (we wanted to do this too but didn’t have the time!) and more- and even go fishing! We saw the grounds for all these activities during our hayride.</p><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
