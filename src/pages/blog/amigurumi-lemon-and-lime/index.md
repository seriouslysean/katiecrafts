<p>My crocheted fruit filled bowl is getting crowded slowly but surely! The bowl is now brimming with</p><strong><a href="/amigurumi-cherries-pattern/">cherries</a></strong><p>, a pear, an orange, a lemon and a lime! (Next up: a plum and a banana!) Learn how to make the happy little lemon and lime right here!</p><p>Crafter June Gilbank has a wonderful blog called</p><a href="http://www.planetjune.com/blog/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Planet June</a><p>where she offers some free crochet patterns, including several amigurumi citrus patterns! I used June’s lemon and lime patterns as the basis for my cute little smiling citrus, with a few tweaks. Please make sure you</p><a href="http://www.planetjune.com/blog/free-crochet-patterns/amigurumi-citrus-collection/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">head over to her site to her original patterns</a><p>, because they are just wonderful! As I was using a different yarn and hook and wanted the lemon a little larger, I had to make a couple changes to mine, which are listed below.</p><h2>Lemon</h2><h3>Materials:</h3><ul><li><p>Yellow worsted weight yarn</p></li><li><p>Lime green worsted weight yarn</p></li><li><p>G6 crochet hook</p></li><li><p>Stuffing/polyfill</p></li><li><p>2 Solid black safety eyes, 6mm (with backs)</p></li><li><p>Embroidery floss, black</p></li><li><p>Embroidery needle</p></li><li><p>Yarn needle</p></li><li><p>Stitch marker</p></li></ul><h3>Pattern Notes:</h3><p>dec = decrease</p><p>inc = increase</p><p>sc = single crochet</p><p>st = stitch</p><h3>Instructions:</h3><p>Start with green yarn.</p><ul><li><p>Round 1: 4 sc into a ring, change to yellow yarn.</p></li><li><p>Round 2: (inc in next st, sc in next st) 2x – (6)</p></li><li><p>Round 3: (inc in next st, sc in next st) 3x – (9)</p></li><li><p>Round 4: (sc in next st, inc in next st, sc in next st) 3x – (12)</p></li><li><p>Round 5: (inc in next st, sc in next st) 6x – (18)</p></li><li><p>Round 6: (sc in next st, inc in next st, sc in next st) 6x – (24)</p></li><li><p>Rounds 7 + 8: sc in each st around – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 9: (inc in next st, sc in next 3 st) 6x – (30)</p></li><li><p>Rounds 10 – 14: sc in each st around – (30)</p></li><li><p>Round 15: (dec, sc in next 3 st) 6x – (24)</p></li><li><p>Rounds 16 + 17: sc in each st around – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 18: (sc in next st, dec, sc in next st) 6x – (18)</p></li><li><p>Round 19: (dec, sc in next st) 6x – (12)</p></li><li><p>Round 20: sc in each st around – (12)</p></li><li><em><p>Take a break from crocheting for a moment. Decide where you want your eyes to be and follow instructions on safety eyes package to attach them. Then use embroidery floss and needle to stitch on a mouth. Make sure it’s secured so it cannot be pulled out. Then add your stuffing, shaping the lemon. Okay, continue crocheting now!</p></em></li><li><p>Round 21: (dec, sc) 4x – (8)</p></li><li><p>Round 22: dec til closed. Finished off. Weave in ends.</p></li></ul><h2>Lime</h2><h3>Materials:</h3><ul><li><p>Lime green worsted weight yarn</p></li><li><p>G6 crochet hook</p></li><li><p>Stuffing/polyfill</p></li><li><p>2 Solid black safety eyes, 6mm (with backs)</p></li><li><p>Embroidery floss, black</p></li><li><p>Embroidery needle</p></li><li><p>Yarn needle</p></li><li><p>Stitch marker</p></li></ul><h3>Pattern Notes:</h3><p>dec = decrease</p><p>inc = increase</p><p>sc = single crochet</p><p>st = stitch</p><h3>Instructions:</h3><ul><li><p>Round 1: 4 sc into a ring</p></li><li><p>Round 2: (inc in next st, sc in next st) 2x – (6)</p></li><li><p>Round 3: inc in each st around – (12)</p></li><li><p>Round 4: (inc in next st, sc in next st) 6x – (18)</p></li><li><p>Round 5: (sc in next st, inc in next st, sc in next st) 6x – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 6: sc in each st around – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 7: (inc, sc in next 7 st) 3x – (27)</p></li><li><p>Round 8 – 10: sc in each st around – (27)</p></li><li><p>Round 11: (dec, sc in next 7 st) 3x – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 12: sc in each st around – (24)</p></li><li><p>Round 13: (sc in next st, dec, sc in next st) 6x – (18)</p></li><li><p>Round 14: (dec, sc in next st) 6x – (12)</p></li><li><p>Round 15: dec til closed. Finished off. Weave in ends.</p></li></ul><p>I’m thinking of finishing off the bowl with a cute little heart shaped peach! What other fruits should I add? What other free amigurumi crochet patterns do you want to see?</p>
