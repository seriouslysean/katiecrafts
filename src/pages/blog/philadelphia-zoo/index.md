<p>This week, Husband and I met up with my friend and her babies for a super fun day at the</p><a href="http://www.philadelphiazoo.org/zoo/Visit-the-Zoo.htm?gclid=CPC_ue60uscCFcsXHwodPyQKsQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Philadelphia Zoo</a><p>! It was pretty sweltering and humid, but it wasn’t too crowded and we still had a great time. Husband took some photos while we were there, so I’m sharing our day in pictures with you!</p><p>Did You Know: The Philadelphia Zoo is the America’s very first zoo?! It’s over 42 acres and more than 1300 animals call it home! Because we went early and it was so hot, many animals were hiding in their habitats, especially the larger cats and such.
