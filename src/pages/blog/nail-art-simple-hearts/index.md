<p>Project: Simple Hearts Nail Art Design</p><strong><br></strong><p>There are an abundance of nail art designs depicting Valentine’s Day, love, romance and more. I decided to keep it simple this week with just a single little heart on each nail. These nails were very easy and quick to do if you want something to celebrate the holiday but don’t have a lot of time to devote to a fancy design!</p><p>Materials:</p><ul><li><p>Clear base/top coat</p></li><li><p>Red nail polish</p></li><li><p>Pink or Lilac nail polish</p></li><li><p>Black nail polish</p></li><li><p>Toothpick(s)</p></li><li><p>Cuticle clippers and nail file</p></li></ul><p>Instructions:</p><p><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="sally hansen double duty" src="./_images/img-0610.jpg" width="510" height="510"></p><p>Clean up your nails and cuticles with your clippers and nail file. Wash and dry hands so they are ready to go! Start with a coat of top coat. I love</p><a title="Sally Hansen Double Duty" href="http://amzn.to/1fzqv5d" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Sally Hansen’s Double Duty</p></strong></a><p>! It doesn’t say fast drying but it totally is. It’s also not super thick and gross like lots of other top coats are.</p><p><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="red" src="./_images/img-0529.jpg" width="510" height="382"></p><p>Next, paint a coat of red polish on each of your ring fingers. I used to leave the ring fingers for last and paint all the others first, but I tend to zone out when I paint my nails and would accidentally paint all of them the same color. I’m trying to teach myself to paint the ring finger first now to avoid doing that!</p><p><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="shiny" src="./_images/img-0535.jpg" width="510" height="510"></p><p>Now paint the other nails with your lilac or pink polish. I picked</p><a title="Sally Hansen Rapid Red" href="http://amzn.to/1eYHPiF" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Rapid Red</p></strong></a><p>and</p><a title="Sally Hansen Lively Lilac" href="http://amzn.to/1g78UlK" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Lively Lilac</p></strong></a><strong><p>Insta-Dri</p></strong><p>by</p><strong><p>Sally Hansen</p></strong><p>. They give nice opaque coats right off the bat, so if you’re in a rush you can pull off just one coat. Let dry completely. Don’t worry about any polish you get on your skin- you can clean that at the end!</p><p><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="skin" src="./_images/img-0534.jpg" width="510" height="510"></p><p>Now for the hearts! I went with red hearts on the lilac nails and a black one on the red nails, just to make them a little different. Use a toothpick dipped in polish to draw a little heart, and then fill it in. Let dry a bit before finishing with a last coat of clear polish.</p><p><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="heartnails" src="./_images/heartnails.jpg" width="510" height="510"></p><figure id="attachment_129" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-129" class="post__figure"><img class="post__image" title="Nail Art: Simple Heart Nail Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com/" alt="finished" src="./_images/img-0547.jpg" width="510" height="382"><figcaption id="caption-attachment-129"><p>Pre-cleaning-polish-off-skin shot</p></figcaption></figure><p>When your nails are totally and completely</p><strong><p>110% dry</p></strong><p>, wash them with soap and water. Gently scrape any rogue nail polish off your skin. This is a better solution for cleaning your nails than to try to target the nail polish bits with a q-tip and nail polish remover- this way there isn’t a chance of you accidentally messing up your perfectly finished nails.</p><p>That’s it- all done!</p><p>What colors would you use for this nail art design? Have any other Valentine’s designs I should try out?</p>
