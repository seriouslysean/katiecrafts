<p>Valentine’s Day is in less than a month! That means I have a few more Manicure Mondays to figure out how I want to wear my nails on February 14th. My first design is a mega simple one, that requires no skill and just a few coats of polish. Try my quick &#x26; easy Valentine’s Day nails if you’re in a rush or looking for something effortless!</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Pink nail polish</p></li><li><p>Black nail polish</p></li><li><p>Red glitter nail polish</p></li><li><p>Clear top coat</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Begin with clean, dry nails. Do one coat of pink on all nails except your accent nail (I picked my ring finger for this.) Let dry.</p></li><li><p>Paint accent nails with black polish. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0424.jpg" alt="Quick &#x26; Easy Valentine&#x27;s Day Nails on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">

        </div></div><ul><li><p>Do a second coat of each if it isn’t opaque enough yet. Let dry.</p></li><li><p>When the black is totally dry, paint a coat or two of the red glitter on top to make it pop!</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Seal in the whole look with clear polish and you’re good to go!</p></li></ul><p>Was that not the easiest Valentine’s nail look ever?!
