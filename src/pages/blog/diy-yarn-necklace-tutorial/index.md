<p>Project: DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial</p><p>Yet another tutorial using t-shirt yarn (woo hoo for upcycling!), this necklace tutorial is incredibly simple to make, and will take you under 10 minutes from getting started to ready-to-wear! Big plus, you can make one to match every outfit you own, so your neck will never be empty or lonely!</p><p>I already made a rust orange one, and this blue one. I also used the same concept, only making all loops the same length instead of differing so that I could make matching bracelets. I’m searching our drawers for more t-shirts I can sacrifice in the name of crafting, as well! Hope you like making these as much as I do!</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>T-shirt yarn</p></li><li><p>Scissors</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Loop the yarn around your neck once to see approximately how long you’d like the shortest loop.</p></li><li><p>Next, mimic that loop with your yarn on a flat surface.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3154.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3155.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Go around a second time, making the next loop slightly larger.</p></li><li><p>Repeat for a third loop.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3156.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3157.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Repeat for a fourth loop, leaving a long tail. Snip yarn.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3158.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3160.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Pinch all layers together and hold up in the air to make sure all the loops hang just the way you want them to.</p></li><li><p>Lay back on the flat surface.</p></li><li><p>With the short tail, making a small knot around all the layers to keep loops together.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3161.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3162.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>With the long tail, tightly wrap around the knot so it isn’t visible, until you run out of yarn.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3164.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3165.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3167.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-3168.jpg" alt="DIY Yarn Necklace Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>When you come to the end of the tail, weave it under the wrapped portion to hold it in place.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
