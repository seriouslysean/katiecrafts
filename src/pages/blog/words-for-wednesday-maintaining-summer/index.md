<p>Today is supposed to bring freezing weather and snow to my area. Fun! It made me wish for the days of Summer- though not really. I hate being hot. Maybe Spring or Fall will be better. 😉 Either way, I found this quote by Henry David Thoreau that I thought would be perfect for today!</p><p><em><strong>“One must maintain a little bit of Summer, even in the middle of Winter.”</strong></em></p><p>I was trying to think of ways to “maintain Summer” and came up with a few ideas!</p><ul><li><p>A beach smelling candle can set the mood</p></li><li><p>An indoor picnic will make it feel like a day outside</p></li><li><p>Have a summery cocktail with an umbrella</p></li><li><p>Hit up an indoor swimming pool or jacuzzi</p></li><li><p>Grill things indoors and have a BBQ with friends</p></li><li><p>Watch movies about the Summertime while giving yourself a pedicure</p></li><li><p>Take a trip to somewhere warmer</p></li></ul><p>What ways are you keeping Summertime alive?</p>
