<p>The</p><a href="/quick-easy-valentines-day-nails/">last Valentine’s day nail art look</a><p>I shared was a very simple red glitter polish. I went a step further this time and used actual glitter in the design! I was going to make little hearts and stuff, but I decided this look was classier and more versatile. Besides, I can’t say no to glittering Valentine’s Day nails!</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Nude/Pink nail polish</p></li><li><p>Fine iridescent glitter in pink (I used</p><a href="http://amzn.to/1UOIdGZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Martha Stewart’s Cotton Candy</a><p>)</p></li><li><p>Clear nail polish</p></li><li><p>Pink nail gems</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Starting with clean, dry nails, paint one coat of pink polish on every nail. Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Do a second coat of pink on every nail</p><strong><p>except</p></strong><p>the accent nails (I picked my ring fingers for these). Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>While the others are drying, paint the accent nails (one at a time) and immediately sprinkle with glitter so that it sticks! Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>On the the thumbs, using either the clear or the pink polish, paint a thick french tip and immediately cover it in glitter. Let dry.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-0704.jpg" alt="Glittering Valentine&#x27;s Day Nails, tutorial from Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><ul><li><p>When all nails are dry, seal in look with clear top coat, skipping the glitter portions if you like. I skipped the glitter so that it would stay extra sparkly!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-0705.jpg" alt="Glittering Valentine&#x27;s Day Nails, tutorial from Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><ul><li><p>While the nails are still wet, add a nail gem to the bottom center of each non-accent nail.</p></li><li><p>Enjoy your sweet Valentine’s manicure… and your Valentine’s Day!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-0709.jpg" alt="Glittering Valentine&#x27;s Day Nails, tutorial from Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><p>Don’t forget to check out Valentine’s nail looks from the past! There are cute ones from</p><a href="/xoxo-valentines-nail-art/">2015</a><p>and</p><a href="/valentines-glitter-design/">2014</a><p>!</p><p>Which nail art design do you like better for Valentine’s Day- this look or</p><a href="/quick-easy-valentines-day-nails/">my last look</a><p>? How will you be wearing your nails that day?</p>
