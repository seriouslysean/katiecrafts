<p>I know the warm weather is creeping up on us, but I’m still completely obsessed with tights! In fact, I have a few pairs that I received from Santa that I have been saving for the warmer weather, to pair with a fun skirt or dress. The same goes for fun leggings paired with ankle boots. Lately, I’m finding myself planning full outfits based on which tights or leggings I want to wear. Here are some amazing pairs of tights and leggings I found and really love!</p><p><a title="Urban Outfitters" href="http://www.urbanoutfitters.com/urban/index.jsp" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Urban Outfitters</a></p><p>is super close to my apartment (dangerously close, actually.) The amount of fun and colorful leggings there is just crazy. They have tons of tights, too! The Betsey Johnson ones above are my fave.</p><p><a href="http://www.hm.com/us/product/17696?article=17696-A" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full post__image" alt="Patterned Tights at H&#x26;M; My Current Obsession: Tights &#x26; Leggings on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/screen-shot-2014-04-04-at-5-14-11-pm.png" width="332" height="438"></a></p><p>Another store (right next to Urban, actually) where I stock up on tights at is</p><a title="H&#x26;M" href="http://www.hm.com/us/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">H&#x26;M</a><p>. I have several pairs of sweater tights that I wore all winter long, and now I’m ready to grab new patterned sheer tights for the summer. Since I love all things chevron, I obviously need the pair above!</p><p><a href="http://store.americanapparel.net/sheer-luxe-small-bow-shapes-pantyhose_rsaphhshbw" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full post__image" alt="Sheer Bow Pantyhose at American Apparel; My Current Obsession: Tights &#x26; Leggings on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/screen-shot-2014-04-04-at-5-19-43-pm.png" width="285" height="415"></a></p><p>I don’t typically shop at</p><a title="American Apparel" href="http://store.americanapparel.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">American Apparel</a><p>, but I will venture in sometimes for their giant wall of tights. The ones with the little bows are totally worth it. So cute!</p><p><a href="http://www1.macys.com/shop/product/hue-polka-dot-control-top-tights?ID=1091919&#x26;CategoryID=40546&#x26;LinkType=#fn=COLOR%3DPurple%26HOSIERY_TYPE%3DLeggings%3B%3BTights%26sp%3D1%26spc%3D9%26ruleId%3D62%26slotId%3D8" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full post__image" alt="Hue Polka Dot Tights at Macy&#x27;s; My Current Obsession: Tights &#x26; Leggings on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/screen-shot-2014-04-04-at-5-27-58-pm.png" width="228" height="391"></a></p><p>These Hue polka dot tights are on sale at</p><a title="Macy&#x27;s" href="http://www.macys.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Macy’s</a><p>right now, so I may have to head over there this weekend and see if there are any pairs left in stock!</p><p><a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/177015599/leggings-skull-print-bright-printed?ref=shop_home_active_5" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full post__image" alt="Lavender and Ash Leggings; My Current Obsession: Tights &#x26; Leggings on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/il-570xn-555456082-pvu5.jpg" width="400" height="600"></a></p><p>Another pair of leggings that I really love and are totally unique come from a shop on</p><a title="Etsy" href="https://www.etsy.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Etsy</a><p>called</p><a title="Lavender and Ash Leggings on Etsy" href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/lavenderandashdesign?ref=l2-shop-info-name" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Lavender and Ash Leggings</a><p>.
