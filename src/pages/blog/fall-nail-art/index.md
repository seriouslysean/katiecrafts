<p>We went to a Fall festival over the weekend and it really got me in the spirit of the season! As if I really needed any more help… 😉 Husband just bought me a six pack of fun Fall colored nail polishes by Kleancolor Nail Lacquer so I decided to try them all out and design a little fall nail art!</p><p>The colors included in the pack from</p><em><a href="http://amzn.to/1Vzywv8" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kleancolor Nail Lacquer</a></em><p>are garnet red, dark brown, americano, copper, gold bright and pearl silver. I used all but the silver in this tutorial.</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Clear base/top coat</p></li><li><p>Nail polish from above set, or other polish in shades of dark red, dark brown, glitter copper, glitter gold and glitter light brown</p></li><li><p>Red nail gems</p></li><li><p>Nail art brush</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Begin with clean, dry nails. Paint a coat of sheer base coat on nails and let dry.</p></li><li><p>Paint all fingers that are NOT accent nails with the garnet red polish. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-1.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-2.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Paint all accent nails with dark brown. Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Do a second coat of each if necessary, and let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Pick your first shade for leaves and dip your nail art brush in! Draw whatever shape leaf you like. Draw a few. Draw them on each of your accent nails. Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Continue with the other shades, drawing different leaves all over the accent nails until you are happy with them.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-6.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-7.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Use the other shades to add depth and don’t forget about the stems! Let dry.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-8.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><ul><li><p>Next up, dip your nail art brush in whichever shade you liked best to draw three lines on your middle fingers. I picked the copper! Add a red nail art gem at the tip to make it look like a burst. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-9.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-10.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Seal in your look with a clear polish. Let dry. Clean up any rogue polish from your skin and enjoy your fall look!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/fall-nail-art-11.jpg" alt="Fall Nail Art Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><p>Here are Husband and I at the Fall Festival! Aren’t we cute?</p><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-2354.jpg" alt="Midtown Fall Festival Philadelphia; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="960" height="960"></p><p>Later this week I’ll be heading to Manhattan for New York Comic Con!
