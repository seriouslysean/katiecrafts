<p>Last year, I had a very cute</p><a title="Nail Art: Easter Design 2014" href="/nail-art-easter-design/"><strong><p>nail art design for Easter</p></strong></a><p>that was reminiscent of a little basket with flowers and decorated eggs. This year, I went for a different Easter staple:</p><a title="Chocolate Covered Easter Peeps" href="/chocolate-covered-easter-peeps/">Peeps</a><p>!</p><p>Besides my grandmother, no one in my family really likes Peeps all that much, but I still always put them in our Easter baskets. They are just so cute! I obviously had to use them in my holiday design.</p><p>I wasn’t planning on getting a manicure this week, but Husband and I had an hour to kill before a tour yesterday with nothing to do, so I decided to get a fun spring-y color on my nails as the base for a nail art I could do later. Plus, my cuticles were so dry and gross- they really needed the love.</p><p>I picked</p><a title="OPI " sit="" under="" the="" apple="" tree&#x26;#x26;#x26;#x26;#x26;#x22;="" on="" amazon&#x26;#x26;#x26;#x26;#x26;#x22;="" href="http://amzn.to/1GFtCIY" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>OPI’s “Sit Under The Apple Tree” green</p></strong></a><p>, which came out lighter than I thought it would, but is still really fun. Plus it’s shimmery, which I love. You can use whatever color or brand of polish you like for this, though!</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Nail polish of your choice for base coat</p></li><li><p>Acrylic paint in: white, black, teal, purple, pink and yellow</p></li><li><p>Large and small dotting tools</p></li><li><p>Clear top coat</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>With clean dry nails, do one to two coats of your preferred base coat shade and let dry.</p></li><li><p>Put a dab of each color on your palette.</p></li><li><p>Put a dab of white beside each color.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-1.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-2.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Mix the white in to each to make it lighter and give it more “pop” on your nails!</p></li><li><p>Use your largest dotting tool dipped in the first color to make a bunny body and adjoining body head on your ring finger.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-4.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-5.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Next, use the opposite side/smaller dotting tool in the same color to make two bunny ears.</p></li><li><p>Repeat using the other colors on your other ring finger and both thumbs. Let dry. Don’t worry about the paint looking stippled- your clear coat will smooth it out.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Use your smallest dotting tool dipped in black paint to make little eyes and noses on the bunnies. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-main.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-7.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Seal in your PEEPS! look with a clear top coat and let dry. Done!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-9.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><p>SO CUTE!!! I love this design so much and can’t wait to show it to the kids on Easter. How will you be decorating your nails for the holiday? Peep peep!</p><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/peeps-nail-art-8.jpg" alt="Easter Nail Art: Peeps! by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p>
