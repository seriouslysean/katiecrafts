<p>Last week, Husband and I went on a guided tour of the</p><a title="Philly Magic Gardens" href="http://www.phillymagicgardens.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Philadelphia’s Magic Gardens</p></strong></a><p>. I’ve passed it probably a thousand times in the many years I’ve lived here, but somehow never visited. What a shame! The mosaics were SO awesome, and I can’t wait to go back and explore on my own to get more photos. Still, the hundred pics we took during the tour will probably do for now. 🙂</p><p>One man- award-winning artist Isaiah Zagar- is responsible for over 200 mosaic walls in Philly and around the world. While on the tour, Isaiah dropped by and said hello to everyone, which was pretty awesome. His mosaics are truly spectacular, and not your typical tile! There are dishes, bicycle wheels, broken down mirrors and more within the walls.
