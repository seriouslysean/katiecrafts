<p>For whatever reason, the last week straight I’ve been singing pretty much nothing but songs from 90’s boy bands in my head. And by ‘in my head’ I mean out loud as I work, as I fold clothes in our building’s laundry room, and as I write this post. And now YOU will have them stuck in your head, too! So hah!</p><p>Because finding the ridiculous music videos of the songs on my playlist was so much fun last time, I’m doing the same today. Especially because they are 90’s videos! The clothes, the hair, the ridiculous situations- you just can’t peel your eyes away from watching! I used to know these videos inside and out, and still know the songs that well. Hope you enjoy these as much as I do!</p><p>I don’t know if NKOTB would be considered a 90’s boy band since they began in the 80’s, but ‘Step By Step’ was released in 1990, so that’s good enough for me and this playlist. Happy Saturday!</p>
