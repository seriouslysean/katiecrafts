<p><br></p><p>Project: DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny</p><p>Easter kind of snuck up on me this year! It’s just over a week away and I haven’t really posted anything for it yet. Whoops! I knew I had to get at least one Easter themed DIY on the blog, and I knew it had to be something QUICK and EASY. This little Pom Pom Bun is the perfect solution!</p><blockquote><p><em>Please Note: While totally adorable, this bun is best served as a decoration and NOT a children’s toy. The tiny eyes, whiskers and other bun parts can be pulled off by little hands, and you don’t want anyone to choke on anything!</em></p></blockquote><p>If you have a pom pom maker, you may absolutely use that instead of your hands to make a much prettier pom pom. For a 5 minute bunny, I thought my hands were a fine tool. 🙂</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>White or ivory yarn</p></li><li><p>White or ivory felt</p></li><li><p>White or ivory thread</p></li><li><p>Needle</p></li><li><p>Black beads for eyes</p></li><li><p>Covered button nose (red or pink)</p></li><li><p>Scissors</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>First, make the small pom pom by wrapping yarn around two fingers until you think it’s a sufficient amount for a bunny head.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0913.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0914.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Then slowly slide the yarn off your fingers, keeping it “looped.” Use the loose end to tie the yarn together at the middle.</p></li><li><p>Once secured, use your scissors to cut through one whole looped side, then the other.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0915.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0916.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Use fingers to fluff out pom pom and snip pieces that are longer to make it uniform.</p></li></ul><p>Not yet snipped to perfection!</p><ul><li><p>Make your larger pom pom by wrapping yarn around four fingers, following the instructions above until you have a bunny body.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0918.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0919.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Make sure you like the way both pom poms look!</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Fold over a small square of felt and cut out a bunny ear. This will give you two ears.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0921.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0922.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0925.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Use your needle and thread to sew the button nose to your small pom pom.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0926.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0928.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Next, sew on the eyes, and lastly the ears.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-0930.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><ul><li><p>Double up your thread and cut ten inches of it. Thread it through your needle, but don’t knot it, so that there are now five inches/four strands.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0931.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0932.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>These are your whiskers. Sew them right through the nose once, and back once. This will give you about an inch of whiskers on each side.</p></li><li><p>Use your scissors to snip the loop and your fingers to separate them.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/img-0933.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><ul><li><p>Now it’s time to attach the head and body! Just use your needle, thread and novice sewing skills to sew the two together.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0935.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-0941.jpg" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><p>Enjoy your cute little pom pom Easter Bunny decoration! Isn’t he adorable!?</p><p><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/bunny-hop-hop.png" alt="DIY Pom Pom Easter Bunny Tutorial by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="980" height="980"></p><p>What fun DIYs do you plan on doing this Easter?
