<p>There are a few holidays I absolutely insist on celebrating because of their sheer importance in this world.</p><em><p>National Chocolate Day</p></em><p>is obviously one of them. While away on our one-year-anniversary-trip this past weekend, we stopped in a little shop that had a whole section devoted to</p><a title="Milka" href="http://www.milka.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Milka</a><p>chocolate bars and other items (surprisingly a Kraft Food’s brand, unsurprisingly not as delicious here as in Europe, but still more wonderful than other chocolates). I bought a couple bars to save for today so that I could celebrate this most holy day! Happy National Chocolate Day, one and all!</p><p>How will you be celebrating?</p>
