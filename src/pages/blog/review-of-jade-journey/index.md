<p>Review of Jade Journey on Etsy</p><p>A couple of weeks ago, I revealed my total obsession with all things anchors! In my</p><a title="Anchor’s Away: My Etsy Picks" href="/anchors-away-etsy-picks/"><strong><p>“Anchors Away”</p></strong></a><p>Etsy treasury, I included this really adorable Key Fob by a shop named</p><a title="Anchor Key Fob by Jade Journey" href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/111253605/seersucker-anchor-monogram-wristlet-key" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Jade Journey</p></strong></a><p>. As a ‘thank you’ for including her, the shop owner, Dawn, sent me my very own personalized fob!</p><p>First of all, this is the very first thing with my new initials on it! As you may know, I was married back in October. However, I didn’t officially change my last name until just a few weeks ago. When Dawn asked me what initials I wanted for the monogram, I was quite excited to be able to use my brand new ones!</p><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">

        </div></div><p>She also asked me what color seersucker I wanted. She had a bunch to choose from, but I inevitably went with the mint/aqua duo (#10)- since mint is my current favorite color. I think with the navy lettering it looks completely perfect together!</p><figure id="attachment_3240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-3240"><a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/jadejourney" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full post__image" src="./_images/il-570xn-558802734-5wxq.jpg" alt="Review of Jade Journey on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="570" height="321"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-3240"><p>photo credit: jade journey</p></figcaption></figure><p>The fob is nice and sturdy and definitely very well made. I know I’ll be using it for quite a long time- and it’s much easier to find my keys in my gigantic Summer bag now, too. 😉 This would be such a great gift idea for a gaggle of bridesmaids or matching family ones in different colors!</p><p><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/img-4048.jpg" alt="Review of Jade Journey on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="883"></p><p>Pop on in to</p><a title="Jade Journey on Etsy" href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/jadejourney" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Jade Journey on Etsy</p></strong></a><p>to see more of her monogrammed key fobs as well as wristlets, clutches, belts and more! And thanks again, Dawn! It was so very sweet of you to send me such a great gift!</p>
