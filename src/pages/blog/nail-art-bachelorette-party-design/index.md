<p>I mentioned</p><a title="Sunday Funday: Issue 6" href="/sunday-funday-issue-6/">last weekend</a><p>that I was in Atlantic City for my friend’s bachelorette party! I also said that I did a fun nail design that was pretty perfect for the occasion, and that I’d share it this week! Well, here we are! Hope you like it as much as I do.</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Clear base and top coats (not pictured)</p></li><li><p>Black glossy nail polish</p></li><li><p>Pink clear-glitter nail polish</p></li><li><p>Pink solid-sparkly nail polish (You’ll see the difference below!)</p></li><li><p>Gold clear-glitter nail polish</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>After cleaning your nails, do a quick clear base coat on your nails.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" alt="Bachelorette Party Nail Art Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/bach-nails-5.jpg" width="637" height="637"></p><ul><li><p>When the clear is dry, paint your ring fingers with one coat of the pink solid-sparkly nail polish. I used</p><a title="Sally Hansen Pink Fast" href="http://amzn.to/1mmJPGQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sally Hansen Insta-Dri in Pink Fast</a><p>. When it’s dry, do a second coat.</p></li><li><p>Next up, do one coat of a nice glossy black polish. My favorite is</p><a title="Maybelline Color Show in Onyx Rush" href="http://amzn.to/1dIzSUb" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Maybelline Color Show in Onyx Rush</a><p>. After the first coat is</p><strong><p>totally dry</p></strong><p>, do a second. Let that one dry too!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" alt="Bachelorette Party Nail Art Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/bach-nails-1.png" width="637" height="637"></p><ul><li><p>Let all coats</p><strong><p>totally 100% dry!</p></strong><p>Now it’s time for glitter! Do one coat of pink clear-glitter over the pink nail.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" alt="Bachelorette Party Nail Art Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/bach-nails-2.png" width="637" height="637"></p><ul><li><p>Pick two nails you want to also do some pink glitter on, and give it a little ombré look with ‘layers’ of glitter.</p></li><li><p>On the remaining nails, do french tips with the gold glitter polish. Repeat glitter coats for extra sparkle!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-full post__image" alt="Bachelorette Party Nail Art Design by Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" src="./_images/bach-nails-4.png" width="497" height="614"></p><ul><li><p>Finish off with a glossy clear top coat. Let dry and you’re ready to party!</p></li></ul><p>I especially loved the black glossy nails with the gold glitter. It looked like I dipped them in gold foil! I will certainly be using this style again. Enjoy your hot pink and black sassy glitter bachelorette party nails!</p>
