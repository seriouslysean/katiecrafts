<p>If you’re reading this, I’m still in New Orleans! I come back tonight, though (womp womp!) In the meantime, here is a simple little design I made in honor of my Katie Crafts logo! It’s got the navy, it’s got the pink, it’s got the stacked hearts. Maybe next time I’ll add the “K”! 😉</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Clear top and base coat</p></li><li><p>Navy blue nail polish</p></li><li><p>Dusty rose nail polish</p></li><li><p>Toothpick or dotting tool</p></li></ul><p>I used</p><a title="Essie After School Boy Blazer" href="http://amzn.to/1jQWr9O" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Essie’s After School Boy Blazer</a><p>for the blue polish, and</p><a title="Essie Sand Tropez" href="http://amzn.to/1l1jRax" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Essie’s Sand Tropez</a><p>for the pink.</p><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Start with clean, manicured nails. Do one coat of clear base coat.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/img-0889.jpg" alt="Nail Art: Katie Crafts Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="883"></p><ul><li><p>When dry, do one to two coats of the blue nail polish. If it’s a thick/opaque polish, you may only need one coat! It will depend on your brand. Let them dry 100%!</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/img-0891.jpg" alt="Nail Art: Katie Crafts Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="683"></p><ul><li><p>Using your dotting tool or toothpick, draw two small hearts on top of each other on each of your ring fingers.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/img-0892.jpg" alt="Nail Art: Katie Crafts Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="854"></p><ul><li><p>Let dry!</p></li><li><p>Use a clear top coat to seal your nails. Let dry, and clean up any extra polish on the skin once dry.</p></li></ul><p><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/img-0895.jpg" alt="Nail Art: Katie Crafts Design on Katie Crafts; http://www.katiecrafts.com" width="663" height="663"></p><p>That’s it for this mega easy Katie Crafts logo design nail art tutorial! Hope you liked it!</p>
