<p>We had a snowstorm on Friday (which also happened to be the first day of Spring!) Thankfully, it warmed up a little over the weekend and melted a good amount of it. Hopefully the Spring decides to stick around for awhile. In anticipation, I found several Springy nail art designs I want to try, starting with this one! Colorful, isn’t it?</p><p>This design is super easy. All you do is make stripes in different colors. You can use what ever colors you like- they don’t even have to be shades you’d find in a rainbow! Here is what I used to complete the look.</p><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Acrylic paint in: red, yellow, purple, blue and teal (white and black are pictured but where not used)</p></li><li><p>Clear top coat</p></li><li><p>Medium nail art brush</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Place a dab of all colors you plan on using on a plate or paper in front of you.</p></li><li><p>Starting with clean dry nails, dip nail art brush in red, and make a line across each of your tips.</p></li><li><p>Clean the brush with water, and dip it in the yellow. Follow right underneath the red line.</p></li><li><p>Repeat process with purple, blue and teal paints. Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Seal in look with one to two coats of clear top coat. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
