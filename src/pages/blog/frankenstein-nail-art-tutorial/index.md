<p>Halloween is under a week away! My last nail design for this season is my favorite so far. It’s a spooky twist on a french tip and was very easy to do. Hope you enjoy my Frankenstein nail art!</p><br><h2>Materials:</h2><ul><li><p>Clear top/base coat</p></li><li><p>White nail polish</p></li><li><p>Green shimmer nail polish</p></li><li><p>Black nail polish</p></li><li><p>Nail art gems for eyes</p></li><li><p>Nail art brush</p></li></ul><h2>Instructions:</h2><ul><li><p>Beginning with clean, dry nails, do a coat of clear and let it dry.</p></li><li><p>Paint your accent nails (I picked my ring fingers and thumbs) with a coat of white. Let dry.</p></li><li><p>Paint the rest of your nails with a white french tip. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-4231.jpg" alt="Frankenstein Nail Art Tutorial on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-4232.jpg" alt="Frankenstein Nail Art Tutorial on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Use the green nail polish to completely cover all white polish. The white will help the green POP!</p><em><p>Let dry completely</p></em><p>.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Do a second coat of green if needed. Let dry.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Use the nail art brush dipped in black nail polish to draw a line separating the bottom of the french tip and your nail on each regular nail. Draw some hair and a line for the mouth on the accent nails. Let dry.</p></li><li><p>Go back over the lines to darken them, and make small stitches on each. Let dry.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-4238.jpg" alt="Frankenstein Nail Art Tutorial on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div><div class="post__gallery-item">
          <img class="post__gallery-image post__image" src="./_images/img-4240.jpg" alt="Frankenstein Nail Art Tutorial on Katie Crafts; https://www.katiecrafts.com" width="600" height="400">
        </div></div><ul><li><p>Use the white nail polish to make small eyes (that are slightly bigger than the gems) on each Frankenstein face. Let dry.</p></li><li><p>Use clear polish to seal in your look. Before the clear dries, stick the rhinestone gems on the white dots to make the eyeballs.</p></li></ul><div class="post__gallery"><div class="post__gallery-item">
