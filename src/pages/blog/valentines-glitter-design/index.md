<p>Project: Valentine’s Glitter Nail Art Design</p><p>While looking for ideas for my Valentine’s Day nail art for next week, I stumbled upon the design I did for my nails last V-Day. You can tell it’s from 2013 since I have no wedding band on my ring finger yet! The design is easy and the glitter polish gives it a little more sparkle!</p><p>Materials:</p><ul><li><p>Clear base/top coat</p></li><li><p>Red nail polish</p></li><li><p>White nail polish</p></li><li><p>Black nail polish</p></li><li><p>Glitter polish</p></li><li><p>Toothpick or dotting tool</p></li></ul><p>Instructions:</p><ul><li><p>First, I painted a layer of clear base coat on my nails and let it dry.</p></li><li><p>Next, I painted two layers of red polish on each nail, letting it dry in between.</p></li><li><p>When those were all dry, I moved on to my hearts. You can draw hearts on all your fingers or just a few for accent. I chose two on each hand. To make the hearts, I used a toothpick (or dotting tool) to create little hearts using the</p><strong><p>black polish only.</p></strong><p>Let dry.</p></li><li><p>When the black is done drying, you’ll draw inside the heart with the white polish and toothpick. This will make it look like it’s outlined in black. So cute!</p></li><li><p>When the white is finally all dry, use your favorite glitter polish. I like something a little on the clear side so you can see the nail art underneath, like</p><a title="Snow Globe Nail Polish by China Glaze" href="http://amzn.to/1c7QMp1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><p>Snow Globe by China Glaze</p></strong></a><p>or something else clear-ish.</p></li><li><p>To finish off your look, lock it in with a clear top coat.</p></li><li><p>Let dry, and enjoy!</p></li></ul><p>Hope you liked this Valentine’s Glitter Design! I’ll post another heart themed nail art design next week!</p>
