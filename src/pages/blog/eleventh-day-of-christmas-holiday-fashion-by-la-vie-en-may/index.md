Today’s 12 Days of Christmas post is by guest fashion and beauty blogger, May of</p><a title="La Vie en May" href="http://www.lavieenmay.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">La Vie en May</a><p>! Check out her blog when you’re done reading her holiday picks below!</p></blockquote><p>Holidays are coming up, do you know what you’re going to wear? Before you start scrambling and pulling your hair out, check out these 4 head-to-toe looks I curated for this holiday season! Whether you prefer pants or dresses, extravagant or simplistic, there’s something for everyone. Alright, let’s start…</p><h2>Look #1: Classic Black Dress</h2><p>Black never goes out of style. It’s a timeless color that’s always chic and fashionable. Plus, its’ slimming qualities don’t hurt either! For this look, I suggest a bold pink lip and soft eyes. It’s all about the pinks this year. Don’t be afraid to make it pop!</p><p>Makeup Pairing:</p><p>Products used: em waterliner in ‘chocolate dream’ &#x26; ‘bronze kiss’, Elizabeth Mott Smooth Shadow in ‘pearl’, and Urban Decay Glide-On Lip Pencil in ‘Adrenaline.’</p><h2>Look #2: Red Dress</h2><p>Red is an iconic color for the holidays and it looks fabulous on anyone. Stand out with a bright red or tone it down with a darker hue. A red dress goes well with neutral tones. To complement the dress, wear a muted, dark red lip.</p><p><a href="https://i1.wp.com/www.katiecrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/holiday-mag-047.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/holiday-mag-047.jpg" alt="holiday mag 047" width="663" height="1057"></a></p><p>Makeup Pairing:</p><p>The palette shown is the em cosmetics Day Life Palette.</p><p><a href="https://i1.wp.com/www.katiecrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/makeup.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/makeup.jpg" alt="makeup" width="663" height="592"></a></p><p><a href="https://i2.wp.com/www.katiecrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/makeup-look4.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-medium post__image" src="./_images/makeup-look4.jpg" alt="makeup--look4" width="663" height="767"></a></p><h2>Look #3: Monochrome</h2><p>Who says you always need to wear a dress to a holiday party?
