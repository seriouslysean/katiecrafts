Happy Friday! Today, February 7th, marks the

**50th anniversary**

of the Beatles first visit to the US! Thus began the Beatles Invasion! What better way to honor the day than with a fun print with their lyrics on it? It would also serve as a lovely gift for your Valentine!

This design was created in Illustrator (with the help of my patient Husband). The lyrics were the easiest part to choose. We walked down the aisle at our wedding to “All You Need is Love.” Done! Next was the background. I’m currently addicted to all things chevron, so that’s what we decided to use for the background of this design. I also chose some ridiculous(ly awesome) fonts that were free from

[**Font Squirrel**](http://www.fontsquirrel.com/ "Font Squirrel")

. Next came the colors- it was hard to choose just a few. I loved it in at least a dozen different hues! Inevitably, I kept it simple with just three. It’s available in these lovely colors: indigo, pink and teal.
