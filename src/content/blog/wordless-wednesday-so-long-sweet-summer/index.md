Doesn’t this photo say it all? Lucky enjoys a last shred of Summer: corn on the cob, freshly buttered and hand-served via his Papa.

The end of Summer always makes me think of this Dashboard Confessional song:

_So long sweet summer,_

_I stumbled upon you and gratefully basked in your rays._\
_So long sweet slumber._\
_I fell into you now you’re gracefully falling away._

Ahh, Dashboard. I think I’ll whip out my old albums and reminisce now.

What ways will you enjoy your last bit of Summer? Are you happy or sad to see the heat leave and the brisk air roll in?
