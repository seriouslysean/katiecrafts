## Materials:

- Clear base/top coat

- Mint green nail polish

- Hot pink sparkly nail polish

- Hot pink nail polish

- Yellow nail polish

- Black nail polish

- Nail art brush

- _Optional:_

  Elmer’s Glue (not pictured), small paint brush and tweezers\*

_\*_

“WHY Elmer’s Glue!?!?,”

_you ask? If you paint the safe-for-your-skin schoolhouse glue on your cuticles and the skin surrounding the nails, you can get messy with the painting/being abstract/using stamps/etc. and not worry about it getting all over your skin. You let it dry completely before you do your normal design and at the end, you use the tweezers to gently peel it off. Easy!_

## Instructions:

I love this design because it’s abstract and you pretty much can’t get it wrong! Just use my design as a guide or inspiration to come up with your own lovely abstract flower!

- Begin with clean, dry nails.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-2.jpg)

- _If you are doing the optional step of Elmer’s Glue, now is the time to paint it on around your nails and let it completely dry before continuing. I put too much on as I didn’t use a paint brush and it took forever to dry. I will definitely be using a paint brush next time!_

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-3.jpg)

- Do one coat of clear base coat and let dry completely.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-4.jpg)

after two coats

- Paint one coat of sparkly pink polish on your accent nail (I picked my ring finger for this one) and let dry.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-5.jpg)

after two coats

- Paint one coat of mint green polish on each of the remaining nails. Let dry.

- Do a second coat of the pink and a second coat of the green on each of the nails.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-6.jpg)

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-7.jpg)

- Use your non-sparkly hot pink polish to make three quick petals. Remember, they do not need to be perfect! I did them on one hand coming out from the bottom of the nail, and on the other hand coming out from the top of the nail. Let completely dry.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-8.jpg)

- Repeat the above step using the yellow nail polish, right on top of the pink to make smaller petals. Let dry.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-9.jpg)

- Make a dot of black polish in the middle of each flower. I found my dot was too “perfectly round” for an abstract look, so I dragged the polish around a little to give it rougher edges! Let dry.

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-11.jpg)

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-10.jpg)

- Use your nail art brush dipped in black polish to create sketchy lines around the petals, accenting and somewhat outlining your flowers! Let dry.

- _Peel off the glue with tweezers, if you did this step!_

- Lock in look with clear nail polish and enjoy!

![Mint & Pink Abstract Floral Nail Art Design by](./flower-nail-art-14.jpg)
