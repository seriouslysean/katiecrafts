Sometimes, things get a little rough and you have trouble remembering how strong you are. Today’s quote is for anyone who is being courageous in the face of tough times. <3

_“Courage isn’t having the strength to go on –_

_it is going on when you don’t have strength.”_

- _-Napoléon Bonaparte_
- Napoléon Bonaparte

Don’t forget: Tough times never last, tough people do!
