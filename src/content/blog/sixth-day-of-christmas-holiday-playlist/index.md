_♫ On the sixth day of Christmas, Katie Crafts gave to me- some indie Christmas songs to si-ing! ♫_

Ah, Christmas music. On the drive home from Thanksgiving every year, we turn our car radio to the 24 hour Christmas music station. I love all the classics and sing along with all of them, but a few that I love I don’t get to hear on the radio- the punk and indie band ones! Most are covers, some aren’t, but all are great. I put together a little playlist of a few of my favorites so you can have fun too.

This list could have been a lot longer, but I kept it to a half hour only. I also ended it with Mariah Carey. Punk or indie? No, no she isn’t. But Mariah’s “All I Want For Christmas Is You” is my favoritest Christmas song and it just had to be on there. Enjoy!

And lastly, here is my favorite band, Jukebox the Ghost, covering “What’s This?” from “Nightmare Before Christmas.” 🙂

What non-traditional Christmas song is your favorite this time of year?
