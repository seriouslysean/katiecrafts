Hello, Monday! If you are an avid Katie Crafts reader, you may have noticed that there was no Sunday Funday post yesterday. For the moment, weekend posts (unless it’s something great that I \*need\* to share \*immediately\*… like a cat photo) will be on hiatus. I love having daily things to share with you guys, but with all the other obligations in my life, and with the beautiful summer days coming to an end soon, I need my weekends to myself! Don’t fret, though! You’ll still see me Monday through Friday, with great posts like the one I have for today: an

**Etsy Back to School Guide**

that you will certainly love!

It feels like just yesterday I was blogging about

[gifts to give the graduates](/grad-gifts-my-etsy-picks/ "Grad Gifts: My Etsy Picks")

in your life, and here we are just a few weeks from starting school up again! Back-to-school shopping with my mom was always my favorite. The art supplies, the colorful notebooks and fun pens, the organizational 5 pocketed binders.. . my Mom loved back to school shopping for me as much as I liked picking it all out. I rarely complained that the summer was ending, because it meant soon I’d get to use my brand new

[Lisa Frank](http://amzn.to/1s2J48m "Lisa Frank Folders on Amazon")

folders! I look forward to the day I get to go back to school shopping with my future kids. I can only hope they enjoy it the way I always did!

If you are looking for some outside-the-box school supplies, Etsy artists have you covered! Above is a treasury I put together of some really great items for your kids (young and old) so they can start the school year off right! And since it’s only the beginning of August, you have time to order these gifts for them so they can proudly present them their first day back! You can find each of the shops from this treasury listed below. Happy shopping!
