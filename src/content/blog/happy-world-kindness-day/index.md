Today, November 13th, is World Kindness Day! All you need to do to participate in this annual holiday is commit to one act of random kindness. One small act of kindness costs nothing and goes a long way! Pay it forward in some small way and you’ve helped make the world a better place, which is what Kindness Day is all about. Here’s a list of ways you can be kind today that won’t cost a dime!

- Hold the door open for someone.

- Smile at or compliment a stranger.

- Help your neighbor with their groceries.

- Let your husband have the last piece of chocolate.

- Call someone you haven’t spoken to in awhile just to say hi.

- Hug your favorite people.

- Offer your seat on the bus to someone else.

- Donate canned goods or gently used clothes to those in need.

- Hide an inspirational note somewhere for others to find.

- Say “Thank You”!

This day was established by the World Kindness Movement on November 13th, 1998 and continues each year with more and more countries joining the celebration. Still, you don’t need to wait until November 13th to unleash your kindness- it’s something you should practice every day!

What small acts of kindness will you commit to?
