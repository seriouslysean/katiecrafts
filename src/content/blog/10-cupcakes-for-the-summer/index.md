Recipes: 10 Cupcakes for the Summer

Cupcakes are one of my favorite things to bake. May is just around the corner, which means my sister’s birthday, my dad’s birthday, my husband’s birthday, his grandmother’s birthday, Mother’s Day and Memorial Day are all almost here. Yeah- May is a very busy month! I searched for recipes I want to use for the upcoming events and found so many, I just had to share!

Seriously, how cute are some of these cupcakes! Watermelon?! Adorable. Also, the Chocolate Greek Yogurt ones are making me drool a little. I can’t wait to give them a try! Here are links to all the above cupcakes so you can find the recipes and try them yourself! Enjoy!
