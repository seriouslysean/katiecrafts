### Did you know…

- The first Aran hand-knit items went on sale in 1935, despite having been around for decades. They were sold in a Dublin store called “The Country Shop,” which was run by a lover of Irish crafts, Dr. Muriel Gahan.

You can learn more about Aran sweaters on the interactive timeline below! The timeline comes from Murphy of Ireland, which is an Irish heritage clothing retailer. The company is based in Co. Donegal in Ireland and has been in business for over 75 years! Make sure you

[check out their website](http://www.murphyofireland.com/)

after you’ve had a go at their timeline!
