I was inspired by Kristin’s announcement that she’d be featuring weekly Flower Friday posts on her blog

_[Aquariann](http://blog.aquariann.com/2015/05/purple-flower-photo-columbine-garden.html)_

, that I just had to dig through old photos and find a good flower to share!

This is a shot from the Philadelphia Flower Show this year, where there was a huge bed of pink tulips. There was a HUGE snowstorm that day, so to step inside the Convention Center and be greeted with TONS of blooming flowers was just about the nicest thing ever. I really loved how huge these tulips were, so I snapped lots of shots of them. Aren’t they lovely?

Share your favorite flower photos below! I’d love to see them!
