[Yesterday](/wordless-wednesday/)

I shared a photo from my trip to Longwood Gardens last week. Husband and I took the day off to make the trip and check out the Christmas light displays before they were gone. Plus, I try to do something special every year on what would be Mom’s birthday, so this was the perfect thing!

We spent the whole day there, wandering around, grabbing lunch, listening to the organ play Christmas music and checking out all the lights once they were lit up! We walked around the entire grounds, stopping here or there to have some of the hot tea we packed to warm up. Later when it was dark, we walked the entire grounds again to see everything sparkly and bright. Here are a ton of photos from our long but wonderful day!
