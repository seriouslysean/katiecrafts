It’s finally here! My recap post with a TON of photos from the 2016 Philly Flower Show! We went on March 7th, so I know I’m a little late- thanks for being so patient. 🙂 The theme this year was “Explore America” and was honoring 100 Years of the National Parks Service. Check out all the photos I took below!

The Flower Show is my favorite annual date with the Husband. We’ve gone every year since we met, so this was the 7th one we attended! The theme this year was probably my least favorite so far, as most of the floral displays were indicative of National Parks that lack in colorful flora and center around trees and underbrush with greens and browns. The displays themselves were put together well, but I prefer things overflowing with colorful flowers. It was still a very nice day, and I did manage to take some great photos!

First up was the Butterfly Experience! I had a few friends land on me, while Husband had even more! They were really digging his shirt.
