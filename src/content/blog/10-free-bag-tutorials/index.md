I love finding blog posts that host a whole array of free patterns all together in one list, so I figured,

_“Why not do that myself?”_

One of the very first things I ever tried to sew was a reversible tote bag, and it’s still one of my favorite ones to date. Here are even more great bag patterns so that you have your pick at bunch!

I scoured the webs and found these wonderful blogs with free bag tutorials for their readers. I can’t wait to try each of these tutorials out, because who can have too many bags?! Be sure to check each of them out below!

1. **[Reversible Tote Bag Tutorial by Katie Crafts](/blog/reversible-tote-bag-tutorial/ "Reversible Tote Bag Tutorial by Katie Crafts")**
2. **[Pleated Pouch Tutorial with Pattern by Lemon Squeezy Home](http://lemonsqueezyhome.com/2010/10/pleated-pouch-tutorial-with-pattern.html "Pleated Pouch Tutorial with Pattern by Lemon Squeezy Home")**
3. **[School Party Satchel by ikatbag](http://www.ikatbag.com/2011/10/school-party-satchels.html "School Party Satchel by ikatbag")**
4. **[Ruffle Zip Pouches by See Kate Sew](http://seekatesew.com/ruffle-zipper-pouch-with-geometric-stitching "Ruffle Zipper Pouch by See Kate Sew")**
5. **[Little Betty Bag by The Mother Huddle](http://www.themotherhuddle.com/little-betty-bag-free-pattern-and-tutorial/ "Little Betty Bag by The Mother Huddle")**
6. **[Bucket Bag by Elvie Studio](http://elviestudio.blogspot.com/2014/05/inspiration-monday_19.html?utm_source=feedburner\&utm_medium=email\&utm_campaign=Feed:+ElvieStudio+%28elvie+studio%29 "Bucket Bag by Elvie Studio")**
7. **[Fairly Bucket Bag by Sew Mama Sew](http://www.sewmamasew.com/2014/09/fairly-bucket-bag-tutorial/ "Fairly Bucket Bag by Sew Mama Sew")**
8. **[The Tohoku Tote by I Am Momma Hear Me Roar](http://www.iammommahearmeroar.net/2011/09/tohoku-tote.html "Tohoku Tote by I Am Mamma Hear Me Roar")**
9. **[The Grandview Bag by The Boy Trifecta](http://www.theboytrifecta.com/2010/10/grandview-bag-formerly-known-as-anthro.html "The Grandview Bag by The Boy Trifecta")**
10. **[Buttercup Bag by Made By Rae](http://www.made-by-rae.com/2009/02/free-buttercup-bag-sewing-pattern/ "Buttercup Bag by Made By Rae")**

What other patterns should I make lists of? Which is your fave bag?
