May is in just a few days, so it’s time to seek out new cute blogs to swap ads with again! We also have some new choices for sponsorships, if that is something you are looking for. Read on below for more details if you want to sponsor or swap ads with

**Katie Crafts**

this month!

As always, Katie Crafts will continue to feature fellow Etsy shops on Wednesdays, but since all the spots are already filled up for May (woo hoo!), we have some new options!

First, if you want to have a product you want to do a giveaway for, or a product you’d like me to review (or both!), you don’t need to wait for a normal Wednesday interview slot to open up! Just contact me with details of what type of giveaway you’d like me to host, or item you’d like me to review and we can come up with other plans together. Same goes for sponsored posts!

Second, I’d love to ad swap with other bloggers for free! Contact me for more info on swapping ads! I’m also in the market for guest bloggers. If you have a project or tutorial you’d love to write about on Katie Crafts, ask me how to become a guest blogger!

Third, there are a couple choices for small businesses to advertise on Katie Crafts!

1. For $5 a month, you can purchase a small ad \[200 x 120] on the sidebar.

2. For $5 a week, you can purchase a large leaderboard ad \[728 x 90] under the first post.
