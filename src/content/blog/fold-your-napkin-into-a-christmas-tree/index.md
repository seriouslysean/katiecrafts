_?On the twelfth day of Christmas, Katie Crafts gave to me…?_

An

[origami](/fifth-day-of-christmas-easy-origami-christmas-tree-diy/)

meets home decor DIY even your toddler could do! Turn your napkins into adorable Christmas trees and wow your guests at Christmas dinner! It only takes a minute and comes out SO CUTE! Learn how to fold your napkin into a Christmas Tree below.

Since I didn’t have a green napkin (actually- I DO have green napkins, but of COURSE I couldn’t find any anywhere for this tutorial!) I used a green piece of fabric. It doesn’t have to be green- it doesn’t have to be Christmassy- it just has to be square! As this wonderful

[YouTube tutorial by Handimania](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=plkWFQ89XyE)

shows you exactly what to do in under two minutes, I will let her teach you below. I’d opt out of the cute bow on top, simply because it’s another step- but feel free to jazz it up like she does!

I was so excited to find this video last year, but Christmas had already passed. I mentally bookmarked it for this season and am glad I remembered to pull it out! It really is the most adorable thing. I’m also all for small details that look time consuming and impressive that really are nothing at all. 🙂
