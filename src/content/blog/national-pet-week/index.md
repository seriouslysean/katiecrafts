Guess what! This week is

[National Pet Week](http://www.petweek.org/ "National Pet Week")

! How purrrfect! From May 4th through May 10th, we should all spend a little extra time loving our fur babies, who spend the entirety of their lives loving us! This Saturday, May 9th, my post will be devoted to all of the pets who make our lives better. I’ll feature photos and little profiles for each of my pets- and yours too! If you want your furry (or scaly, or feathered!) friend to be a featured pet on Katie Crafts, learn more below!

To participate in National Pet Week here on Katie Crafts, all you’ll need to do is send an email by clicking Mabel below, with your favorite photo of your pet attached, and fill out the little bio for him or her! Have two or three pets? I’ll feature them all! The more, the merrier. Let’s see how big and fuzzy we can make this “Katie Crafts Pet Round Up” on Saturday! Send your photo and email before Friday at 5:00PM ET, and you’ll be sure to see your sweet little pet on Saturday’s post. Can’t wait to meet your little buddies!
