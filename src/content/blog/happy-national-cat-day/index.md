October 29th is

[National Cat Day](http://www.nationalcatday.com/)

! The annual holiday is to intended to do one thing: celebrate our furry friends! A day all about cats helps spread the word about kitties that need adopting, rescues that need help at the shelters and gives you a good reason to go cat lady crazy!

Both my kitties are rescues, so a day that promotes finding a loving home for other cats in need sounds like a purr-fect holiday to me! Hope you spend your National Cat Day with your favorite kitty friend(s)! Now enjoy a short clip of Mabel kneading her blanket to death.
