Valentine’s Day is in less than a month! That means I have a few more Manicure Mondays to figure out how I want to wear my nails on February 14th. My first design is a mega simple one, that requires no skill and just a few coats of polish. Try my quick & easy Valentine’s Day nails if you’re in a rush or looking for something effortless!

## Materials:

- Pink nail polish

- Black nail polish

- Red glitter nail polish

- Clear top coat

## Instructions:

- Begin with clean, dry nails. Do one coat of pink on all nails except your accent nail (I picked my ring finger for this.) Let dry.

- Paint accent nails with black polish. Let dry.

![Quick & Easy Valentine's Day Nails](./img-0424.jpg)

![Quick & Easy Valentine's Day Nails](./img-0423.jpg)

- Do a second coat of each if it isn’t opaque enough yet. Let dry.

- When the black is totally dry, paint a coat or two of the red glitter on top to make it pop!

* Seal in the whole look with clear polish and you’re good to go!

Was that not the easiest Valentine’s nail look ever?! I hope you liked it too!
