I love this quote by Walt Disney.

> _“Why worry? If you’ve done the very best you can, worrying won’t make it any better.”_

This is something I should be reminding myself of daily, as I pretty much never stop worrying about every little thing imaginable! Having constant anxiety over everything is pretty awful.

Mostly, I feel like the moment I STOP worrying about something is the moment I’ll get judged for not caring enough, which is seldom true. Still, can’t help that guilty feeling from creeping in all the time. 13 years of Catholic schooling at it’s finest!

I will definitely work on worrying less, and could use some suggestions on what you do to calm your nerves! Meditate? Read? Drink? 😉 Share below!
