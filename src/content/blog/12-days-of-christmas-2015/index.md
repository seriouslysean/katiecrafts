Katie Crafts celebrated the holidays last year with the

_**12 Days of Christmas**_

: twelve days straight of giveaways, free patterns, holiday themed nail art, recipes and more! Everything you need to make a Merry Christmas for those you love. 🙂 Want to participate?

## _**Katie Crafts is looking for sponsors!**_

If you have an Etsy shop or small business and want to sponsor a giveaway on Katie Crafts- wonderful! I’d love to interview you, include photos of your work and host your giveaway for you!

If you are a fellow blogger and would like to do a guest blog for Katie Crafts- awesome! Share your favorite holiday recipe, your picks for Winter fashion, a Christmas DIY or whatever you like!

If you’re an expert at crafting beautiful Etsy treasuries and want to share yours on Katie Crafts- fabulous! Christmas shopping/gift ideas all in one beautiful place? Love it!

12 Days of Christmas will take place from

_December 1st through December 12th_

, giving you plenty of time to ship out a prize before the holidays, make a gorgeous treasury to people to shop, or write a fun guest post recipe/diy/etc. for people to try!

Interested in participating?
