Yippee! Today is

_National Coffee Day_

– my Husband’s favorite holiday! He’s quite the coffee addict… err… lover. We will celebrate this morning with a few cups in our kitchen and later with a FREE cup from Dunkin! All

[**Dunkin Donuts**](http://news.dunkindonuts.com/Press-Releases/DUNKIN-DONUTS-NEW-RAINFOREST-ALLIANCE-CERTIFIED-DARK-ROAST-BLEND-NOW-AVAILABLE-NATIONWIDE-4cd.aspx "Dunkin Donuts")

are giving away a free cup of their new Dark Roast, which sounds pretty great to me!

Don’t like Dunkin? There are other places giving away coffee in honor of the day too!

[](http://news.mcdonalds.com/US/releases/McDonald-s-USA-Perks-Up-The-Nation-With-Free-Coffe "McDonalds")

[**McDonalds**](http://news.mcdonalds.com/US/releases/McDonald-s-USA-Perks-Up-The-Nation-With-Free-Coffe "McDonalds")

is giving a small cup of coffee to each customer during the morning hours.

[](http://www.krispykreme.com/NationalCoffeeDay "Krispy Kreme")

[**Krispy Kreme**](http://www.krispykreme.com/NationalCoffeeDay "Krispy Kreme")

customers can get a free 12 ounce hot coffee.

[](https://www.facebook.com/peets "Peets Coffee and Tea")

[**Peet’s Coffee & Tea**](https://www.facebook.com/peets "Peets Coffee and Tea")

is giving it’s customers a second cup of joe free when they buy the first one.

[](http://store.starbucks.com/starbucks-ethiopia/011028995,default,pd.html\&siteID=NKa3hZyYoHA-y53R2bIBoCxqQx6guebl1A\&cm_mmc=Affiliate-_-NKa3hZyYoHA-_-Evergreen-_-na "Starbucks")

[**Starbucks**](http://store.starbucks.com/starbucks-ethiopia/011028995,default,pd.html\&siteID=NKa3hZyYoHA-y53R2bIBoCxqQx6guebl1A\&cm_mmc=Affiliate-_-NKa3hZyYoHA-_-Evergreen-_-na "Starbucks")

is giving away free tastings of it’s coffee titled “Ethiopia,” which is the birthplace of coffee production! If you buy a bag of said coffee, you also will get an Ethiopia Tasting Cup for free too!

How will you be celebrating National Coffee Day?
