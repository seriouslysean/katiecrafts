I’m a winner! No, not of the Powerball (though that would surely be nice), but of a gift card to

[Benique](http://benique.com/)

.com.

[La Vie en May](http://www.lavieenmay.com/)

had lots of great giveaways going on during the Christmas season, and one was a gift certificate to Benique. I hadn’t heard of them before, but after checking out their products I was in love! When I found out I won $10 towards my purchase, I jumped right on their site and picked out my favorite earrings.

I took about a week to decide. It was between these heart ones and little flower ones, but with Valentine’s coming up I opted for the hearts- and I’m so glad I did! They are SO pretty and sparkly in person!

![Benique Jewelry Review by](./img-0596.jpg)

![Benique Jewelry Review by](./img-0597.jpg)

The packaging was just adorable. They were gently tucked inside a box with tissue paper and what reminds me of pink Easter basket grass.

![Benique Jewelry Review by](./img-0598.jpg)

![Benique Jewelry Review by](./img-0599.jpg)

They had a sweet handwritten note to me along with a special freebie- another pair of lovely earrings! Also included was a little cloth to clean the jewelry.

I was extremely impressed with all their wares and beautiful packaging and will definitely be using them in the future. It would make a great gift to ship right to a friend since the package is already so pretty!

![Benique Jewelry Review by](./img-0601.jpg)

![](./freebie-earrings.jpg)

These heart earrings are my new favorites and I’ve worn them every day this week! They match everything, so why not? 🙂

_Thanks for introducing me to them, May!_
