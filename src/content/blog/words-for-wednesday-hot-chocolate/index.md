Usually my Wednesday posts involve just a quote, or nothing but a pretty photo, but today is a bit different! Husband found the above chart of different ways to spice up your hot chocolate and I found it so fun that I just had to pass it along! He saw someone post it from

[SheKnows](http://www.sheknows.com/ "SheKnows")

and he knew I’d like it- and he was right.

Peppermint hot chocolate (and peppermint mochas- mmmmm!) and Irish hot chocolate are definitely my two favorites in the Winter, but now I just

_have_

to try all these other flavors! Especially if it means I don’t have to leave the house and venture in to the cold to go to the coffee shop for such a drink. For Christmas last year, I bought Husband a latte machine, and in addition to making it’s namesake, it also makes killer hot chocolate. I will certainly be trying these recipe suggestions out in the machine and will let you know which is the best! I think I’ll start out with the Nutella one…. 🙂

Which spiced up hot chocolate strikes your fancy most?
