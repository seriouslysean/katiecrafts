I love looking up at the clouds and seeing shapes and faces, or seeing what looks to be a random smiley made out of rocks on the sidewalk. Finding faces in things that don’t really have faces always makes me smile, so of course when we brought home tomatoes, took them out of the shopping bag and one of them had already been squished- in to a face- I had to snap a photo.

He looks SO HAPPY with his giant mouth! Doesn’t he!? It kind of reminds me of something from a Tim Burton flick. 🙂

Have you ever found a face in something totally unexpected? Share in the comments!
